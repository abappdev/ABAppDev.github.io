Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my tasks across devices?

TaskFlow Pro automatically syncs your data when you're signed in to your account. Ensure you're connected to the internet and using the same account on all devices.

Can I collaborate with my team?

Yes! TaskFlow Pro offers team collaboration features in our Pro plan. You can share projects, assign tasks, and track team progress together.

How do I backup my data?

Your data is automatically backed up to our secure cloud servers. You can also export your tasks manually through Settings â†’ Export Data.

What's included in the Pro subscription?

Pro subscription includes unlimited projects, team collaboration, advanced analytics, priority support, and exclusive productivity features.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage your subscription through your App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android) account settings. Alternatively, contact our support team for assistance.
